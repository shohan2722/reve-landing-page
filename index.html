The Rêvée method

Physician-Led Aesthetic Architecture™

Most consultations at most practices go something like this: you sit down, someone asks what bothers you, you point to your forehead and say "these lines," and fifteen minutes later you're scheduled for Botox.

What you don't get is a comprehensive assessment of everything else that might be contributing to how you look — your facial proportions, your skin quality, your underlying volume, your facial movement patterns, and how they all interact.

Our physician-led team approaches your face the way a skilled architect approaches a building: we study the entire structure before we recommend a single change. We look at what's working. We identify what could be optimized. We understand your goals, your comfort level, and your timeline.

Then — and only then — we create a personalized treatment roadmap built specifically for you. No pressure. No upselling. No one-size-fits-all recommendations.

Common questions

Everything you might be wondering.

On the Consultation

Is the consultation really complimentary? What's the catch?
There is no catch. Your initial consultation is genuinely complimentary — no credit card required, no obligation to book a treatment, no minimum purchase. We offer it this way because we believe the best patient relationships start with trust, not transactions. You come in, we assess your concerns and goals, we build a plan together. What you do next is entirely up to you.
How long does the consultation take?
We schedule 45 minutes for new patient consultations. We use that time to have a real conversation about your concerns, review your skin and facial structure, and develop your personalized treatment roadmap. We don't rush it.
Will I feel pressured to buy something during the consultation?
No. Our consultation process is designed specifically to be pressure-free. We'll share what we recommend and why, but the decision of whether, when, and how to move forward is yours — always. Many of our patients book their first treatment right away. Others go home, think about it, and come back weeks later. Both are perfectly fine.

On Results & Safety

I'm afraid of looking fake or overdone. How do I know that won't happen?
This is the concern we hear most often — and it's exactly why our approach is built the way it is. We specialize in natural-looking aesthetic enhancement. Our team is trained to work with your individual facial anatomy and movement patterns, not against them. Our Botox and filler patients routinely tell us that friends ask if they've been on vacation, not if they've had "work done." Natural results are not a marketing phrase for us — they're a clinical standard.
Is Botox safe? What about fillers and lasers?
All of our treatments are FDA-approved and administered by trained, physician-informed aesthetic professionals. We follow strict medical protocols for every procedure. During your consultation, we will walk you through exactly what to expect, including any associated risks, typical side effects, and what to do if you have questions after your treatment. Safety is not an afterthought here — it is foundational to everything we do.
What if I don't like my results?
Our commitment to your satisfaction doesn't end when you leave the treatment chair. Follow-up consultations are included in your care, and we're here to answer questions and address any concerns throughout your treatment journey. We will never leave you without support.

On Treatments

I don't know what treatment I need. Can you help me figure that out?
Absolutely — and this is actually one of the most important things we do. You don't need to know whether you need Botox or filler, Halo or BBL, microneedling or a peel. That's our job. During your consultation, we'll assess your specific concerns and create a plan that addresses them in the right sequence, with the right treatments. You just need to show up and tell us what bothers you.
How much do treatments cost?
Our treatment fees range from accessible entry-level services like dermaplaning and HydraFacial to comprehensive programs including Halo laser series, Sculptra, facial balancing, and medical weight loss. We'll discuss all relevant pricing during your consultation so you can make an informed decision that aligns with your goals and budget. We never surprise you with unexpected costs.
How soon will I see results?
It depends on the treatment. Botox results typically appear within 3–7 days and peak at two weeks. Filler results are immediate. Laser treatments and skin rejuvenation programs typically show progressive improvement over several weeks to months. During your consultation, we'll set clear, realistic expectations for your specific treatment plan — including timeline, number of sessions, and what the results will look like at each stage.
